The Grand

Sri Lanka’s iconic landmark, The Grand Hotel, is situated in the heart of Colombo, along the seafront and facing the famous Galle Face Green. One of the oldest hotels east of the Suez, The Grand Hotel embraces its rich history and legendary traditions, utilizing them to create engaging, immersive experiences that resonate with old and new generations of travellers alike.

Mansion

Surrounded by sweeping green hills, misty mornings bordered by the longest river in the country, Bourbon Mansion Kandy is your ideal retreat for a truly relaxed and carefree getaway. This resort is designed to capture its rich cultural and historical significance with the use of ‘heritage’ red representing royalty and white spaces symbolizing a modern, chic and sophisticated ambiance.

Lodge

Tucked away in an exclusive sanctuary beyond the legendary rock fortress of our island, Bourbon Lodge welcomes you to Sigiriya – the centerpiece of Sri Lanka’s famed cultural triangle. Surrounded by five man-made ecosystems, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality is a celebrated icon of sustainability that invites you to indulge in true eco-luxury amidst our historic heartlands.
